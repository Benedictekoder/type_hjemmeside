As the title of this page denotes, it`s all about visuals here. More precisely its about capturing light in all its wonderful variants.

Light is the beginning of all beauty

One of my first and dearest memories is of playing in a large field amongst the tall grass gently swaying in the soft afternoon wind, all while being bathe in a warm ray of golden summer sun. I remember stopping playing just to stand and marvel at the beauty of my surroundings. And from that day forward, I have always tried, in various ways, to capture the beauty that lies within creation.
